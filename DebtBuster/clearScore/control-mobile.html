Debt Counselling is a well-designed programme that can save you money and unlock monthly cashflow for you.

More on the process: Debt Counselling is a well-designed programme, resulting in savings for over-indebted consumers. The process entails you to agree to a budget, whereafter the debt counsellor will restructure your debt.
A new financial plan is formulated for you through a lower, consolidated repayment, with legal protection that is negotiated with your credit providers.

Together, these factors save you money and unlock monthly cashflow for you.
