Frequently Asked Questions

How long do the solar festoon lights stay on at night?

Our Solar Festoon Bulb String Lights provide up to 12+ hours of illumination on a full charge. The duration depends on the amount of direct sunlight the solar panel receives during the day.

Pro Tip: For optimal performance, place the solar panel in direct sunlight for at least 6-8 hours daily.

Can I connect multiple solar string lights together?

Yes! You can interconnect up to 30m/100ft (3 sets) on a single solar panel. However, connecting multiple sets reduces the working time per charge, as the power is shared among all bulbs.

Do the lights automatically turn on and off?

Yes! These lights are light-sensitive, meaning they automatically turn on at dusk and turn off at dawn. No need to manually switch them on or off—just set them up and enjoy!

Bonus Feature: A built-in ON/OFF switch allows you to override the automatic function when needed.

Can the solar panel be charged via USB?

Yes! Our Smart Solar Panel includes a USB charging option, allowing you to charge the panel indoors on cloudy days or in low-sunlight areas.

Are the bulbs durable and weatherproof?

Absolutely! Our Solar Festoon Bulb String Lights are designed for outdoor use and built to last:

  • Shatterproof bulbs – Safe, impact-resistant design
  • IP65 Weatherproof rating – Withstands rain, wind, and sun
  • Durable rubber cable – Flexible yet tough for all-weather conditions
